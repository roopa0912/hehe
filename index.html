i love you.

i miss you in the small, ordinary moments — the stove clicking on, the quiet after a laugh, the walk from the door to the sofa. you’ve made my ordinary days glow a little brighter.

if feelings could doodle, they’d draw us as two bears who keep finding each other even when the page flips. you, me, a silly postcard, and the kind of warmth that doesn’t need fixing.

you exist in the background of everything nice. the morning light, a soft hoodie, the sound of tea being poured — they all remind me of you somehow.

sometimes i wish you could peek into my brain just to see how many small moments you live in. you’d roll your eyes, but i’d still tell you anyway.

this is me waving from my side of the day: hi, silly human. you are my favorite person, my best accidental habit, and the calmest chaos i’ve ever loved.
